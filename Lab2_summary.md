# Lab 2 Summary

## Character Classes Implemented
Catcher - hitter plus fielder
Pitcher - most important defensive player, can weaken an opposing players hitting stats
Coach - can boost stamina, hitting stats and pitching stats,
Second baseman - fielder plus high level contact hitter

## Special Abilities
hot streak - temporarily boosts a pitchers stats
motivational speech - can restore health/stamina to a pitcher
steady hand - allows a catcher to boost the stats of their pitcher

## Testing Approach
I tested to see if what I inteded to happen actually happened. For example if I wanted an ability to boost stamina by 10, I tested to make sure the abitily actually applied that boost. I also tested that certain stats cant be unrealistic numbers. For example, stamina should always be above 0 and other stats like averages, ops, slugging should also stay within a certain range.

## Challenges Faced
[What was difficult? How did you overcome it?]
